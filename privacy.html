WorkBlast: Privacy Policy

What personal data we collect and why?

When you create an account with WorkBlast, we collect your email, first name, and last name. These data are necessary for proper app functionality. WorkBlast also collects basic information about your device, such as manufacturer, OS, and OS version in order to display this information back to you for the purpose of session management.

What data do we share with 3rd parties?

WorkBlast does not share your personal data with 3rd parties.

What rights do you have over your data?

You can email support@getworkblast.com and request your data be permanently deleted. WorkBlast will honor the request within 30 days of receipt.
